Major Cities of the Indus Valley Civilization

Harappa

Harappa was one of the major cities of the Indus Valley Civilization, known for its advanced urban planning and architecture.

Mohenjo-Daro

Mohenjo-Daro is renowned for its sophisticated drainage system and the Great Bath, a large public water tank.

Dholavira

Dholavira was an important city known for its large reservoirs and extensive use of water management techniques.

Lothal

Lothal was a significant port city famous for its dockyard, which highlights its role in maritime trade.
